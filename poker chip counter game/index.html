Poker Chip Counter

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four poker chips below. By clicking on a chip you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number -- you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each chip is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game starts, it will change the values of each chip.
